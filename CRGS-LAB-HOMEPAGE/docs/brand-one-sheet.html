Clear space & minimum size

Keep clear space around the mark equal to the height of one stack bar. Minimum legible size: 24px for the mark, 120px wide for the full lockup. Below that, use the mark alone.

Usage

Do

  • Use terracotta as the single accent; keep ink on paper for everything else.
  • Use the white lockup on the kernel dark or any dark photo.
  • Pair Fraunces headlines with mono labels.
  • Give the mark room to breathe.

Don't

  • Recolor the mark outside the brand palette.
  • Stretch, rotate, or add shadows or gradients to the mark.
  • Place the color lockup on a busy or low-contrast background.
  • Substitute different fonts for Fraunces or JetBrains Mono.
