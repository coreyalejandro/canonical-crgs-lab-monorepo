### C. Equipment ($580,000)

#### C.1 Bare-Metal H100 GPU Cluster — $500,000

**Configuration:** 8× NVIDIA H100 GPUs (80GB each)  
**Justification:**  
Real-time adaptive AI systems require low-latency inference (<150ms p99) under concurrent load (10,000 agents). Cloud GPU instances introduce variable latency and are cost-prohibitive at scale ($3-5/hour × 8 GPUs × 24/7 × 36 months = $2.5M+). Purchasing bare-metal H100s provides:

1. **Predictable Latency:** No noisy neighbor effects
2. **Cost Efficiency:** 5-year lifespan amortizes to <$100k/year
3. **Data Security:** On-premises deployment for sensitive participant data
4. **Research Control:** Full access to system metrics for chaos engineering

**Depreciation:** 5-year lifespan; residual value ~$200k after project completion, available for future research.

**Alternative Considered:** Cloud GPUs rejected due to cost and latency variability.

---

#### C.2 High-Availability Server and Networking — $80,000

**Components:**
- 2× redundant servers for database and API gateway ($30k)
- 10GbE networking equipment ($20k)
- Uninterruptible power supply (UPS) ($15k)
- Backup storage (100TB NAS) ($15k)

**Justification:**  
High-availability infrastructure ensures 99.9% uptime for continuous data collection and CHAE monitoring. Redundant servers prevent data loss in case of hardware failure. 10GbE networking supports real-time data streaming from 1,000 concurrent participants.

## Budget Justification Summary

This budget reflects the true cost of conducting rigorous, large-scale, neurodivergent-first AI research with the highest standards of scientific quality, ethical oversight, and community engagement. Key investments include:

1. **Neurodivergent Co-Leadership:** Full-time NCL with equal authority ensures authentic neurodivergent-first governance.

2. **Production-Grade Infrastructure:** H100 GPU cluster enables real-time, low-latency adaptive AI at scale.

3. **Global Reach:** Four-hub design with in-person visits ensures cross-cultural validity and equitable participation.

4. **Open Science:** Significant investment in open-source community, reproducibility, and public dissemination.

5. **Ethical Rigor:** Continuous Human Audit Engine (CHAE), IRB compliance, and participant compensation reflect commitment to ethical research.

6. **Workforce Development:** Training of 4 GRAs, 2 postdocs, and 2 RSEs contributes to diverse STEM workforce.

This budget demonstrates that neurodivergent-first research at scale requires substantial investment, but the scientific and societal returns—validated constitutional AI, inclusive design principles, and a model for participatory research—justify the cost.

## Budget Flexibility and Contingency

The budget includes modest contingency within "Other Direct Costs" and "Miscellaneous" categories. If costs are lower than projected (e.g., cloud services, travel), savings will be reallocated to:

## Comparison to Bootstrapped Pilot (<$1,000)

The bootstrapped pilot demonstrates that the core research protocol can be validated at minimal cost:
- **Bootstrapped:** <$1,000 (N=10, free-tier infrastructure, volunteer audit buddy)
- **Megaproject:** $7,200,000 (N=1,000, production infrastructure, full governance)

This 7,200× cost increase reflects:
- 100× sample size increase
- Production-grade infrastructure (vs. localhost)
- Full-time research team (vs. solo researcher)
- Global multi-site coordination (vs. online-only)
- Continuous safety monitoring (vs. weekly manual review)
- Comprehensive dissemination (vs. OSF preprint)

The bootstrapped pilot de-risks the megaproject by validating instruments, effect sizes, and feasibility before large-scale investment.
